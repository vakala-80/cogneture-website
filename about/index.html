Our Story

Built to Bridge Technology & Business

Cogneture was founded in 2010 by a team of technology veterans who believed that organizations deserved a partner — not just a vendor. We saw a gap between what businesses needed and what the market was delivering: fragmented services, misaligned talent, and consulting that produced decks instead of results.

We built Cogneture to be different. Our integrated model — combining IT services, staffing, and consulting under one roof — ensures that strategy and execution are never disconnected. Every recommendation we make, we can also deliver.

Today, with 500+ professionals and 300+ clients across 7 industries, we are proud to be the trusted technology partner for organizations ranging from high-growth startups to global enterprises.
